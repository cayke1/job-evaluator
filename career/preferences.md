# Job Preferences - Cayke

## Preferred Roles

- Backend developer
- Backend Node.js developer
- Full-stack developer with backend focus
- Software engineer intern
- Junior software engineer
- Entry-level software engineer

Backend-oriented opportunities should be prioritized over frontend-heavy positions.

## Preferred Stack

- Node.js
- TypeScript
- PostgreSQL
- Express
- React
- Next.js

## Preferred Companies

- Startups
- SaaS companies
- AI startups
- Product-focused teams
- Modern engineering teams

## Work Model

Remote is strongly preferred.

Hybrid is acceptable only when:

- The opportunity is exceptionally strong
- The commute/location is reasonable
- The growth potential is high

On-site roles should generally receive lower priority.

## Geography

- Brazil remote opportunities
- International remote opportunities

## Opportunities to Prioritize

Prioritize roles where:

- Real projects matter more than formal experience
- Portfolio evaluation is valued
- Technical ownership is encouraged
- Startup mentality is appreciated
- Product thinking is relevant
- Backend ownership exists
- API and system design exposure is present
- Junior candidates can grow technically

## Opportunities to Avoid or Penalize

Avoid or heavily penalize:

- Senior-only positions
- Positions requiring 5+ years of experience
- Legacy-only stacks
- WordPress-only jobs
- Pure maintenance roles
- Frontend-only positions with minimal backend work
- Non-technical support positions
- Extremely enterprise/legacy environments unless there is exceptional growth potential
- Mandatory on-site positions unless exceptionally strong

## Medium-Priority Opportunities

These can be considered when the overall opportunity is strong:

- Full-stack roles with balanced backend work
- Laravel/PHP positions with modern practices
- Hybrid opportunities with strong growth potential

## Long-Term Career Goals

Cayke's main goal is to become a strong backend/full-stack engineer focused on scalable SaaS products, APIs, startups, and modern product engineering.

Long-term interests:

- Backend systems
- SaaS
- AI tooling
- Scalable products
- Modern web engineering
- Product development
- Startups

Not interested in:

- Pure maintenance careers
- Bureaucratic or non-technical roles
- Frontend-only specialization
- Legacy-only career paths
